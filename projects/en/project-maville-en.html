School Project

MaVille

App to connect citizens, contractors, and the city for real-time public works management.

Back to portfolio

Summary

Command-line and GUI platform that centralizes reports, automates prioritization, and lets contractors respond to projects. Targeted notifications and local persistence.

Design, full development, testing
Java, Maven, JUnit, Javalin (REST), JSON/DAO
2025

Demo Video

Features

Profiles

Resident, Contractor, City (STPM) with dedicated menus and permissions.

Reports

Create issues with neighborhood, street, type, description, and tracking.

Projects

List of ongoing/upcoming works, filter by district and type.

Bids

Contractors submit quotes, manage active projects.

Prioritization

Automatic priority assignment by the city (STPM).

Notifications

Subscriptions/notifications by area and project.

Architecture

Model & Data

Entities: Project, Issue, Resident, Contractor. JSON DAO for local persistence and external Montréal API integration.

Services & Interfaces

Resident/Contractor/City/Notification services. CLI, GUI, and Javalin REST server interfaces.
